About us

Fuego Mexican Grill is a casual-style restaurant dedicated to serving the most authentic and delicious Mexican cuisine. Founded by a husband and wife team from Mexico, each dish is based on family recipies passed down through generations. We are proud to serve food made from the higest quality ingredients that are sure to satisfy!
